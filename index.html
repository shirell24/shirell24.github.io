Vector Illustration: Bieb Malti

Hello there! I am Shirely Ellul, a first year BFA student currently studying Digital Art at the University of Malta, and welcome to my website.

Introduction

This is one of my favourite topics and artworks that i have covered and created so far. It is a vector illustration done on Adobe Illustrator, a graphic design software.

The Artwork

Inspired by Te' fit-tazza and Maltatype, this artwork is meant to capture a simple yet iconic part of Maltese architecture, found in many older towns and cities. The process started off with a simple sketch of the door and composition, in order to simplify and better visualise the final work. After, that I started building the final work using basuc shapes and colours, slowly adding depth and detail
